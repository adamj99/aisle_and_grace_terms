Privacy Policy

Effective:

Aisle & Grace is a local‑first wedding planning app. We respect your privacy.

Data We Store

  • Wedding planning data (tasks, budget, vendors, guests, and timeline) is stored locally on your device using an encrypted app sandbox.
  • Optionally, you can create a private backup to your own Google Drive app data area. This is off by default and only runs when you choose Backup/Restore in Settings.

Data We Don’t Collect

  • No analytics SDKs, no ads, and no selling of data.
  • We don’t collect personal information beyond what’s shown in the app.

Google Sign‑In

If you enable Drive backups, we request Google Sign‑In to upload/download your JSON backup to your Drive’s hidden appDataFolder. You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account settings.

Your Choices

  • You can keep everything local and never sign in.
  • You can delete backups from Google Drive and revoke access at any time.
  • Remove the app to delete local data from your device.
